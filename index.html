About Cypress Andruss

Hi, I'm Cypress Andruss, a passionate and dedicated racer with big dreams. I’m focused on becoming one of the greatest drivers of all time, from Formula 1 to endurance racing like Le Mans and Daytona. I started racing go-karts at the age of 7, and since then, I’ve achieved numerous victories, including being named "King of the Willow" four years ago at the Willow Springs Karting Track. I've had the opportunity to race across the country and have worked with incredible drivers who have helped shape my journey.

I’m driven not only to succeed in motorsports but also to inspire others, especially kids who might not have the same opportunities I’ve had. With faith in God and hard work, I believe that nothing is impossible. I'm here to show young racers the importance of determination, focus, and resilience both on and off the track.
